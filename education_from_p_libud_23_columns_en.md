# `education_from_p_libud_23.csv` — column definitions (English)

This CSV is generated by `extract_education_from_p_libud_23.py` from the CBS/LAMAS file `p_libud_23.xlsx` (sheet: `נתונים פיזיים ונתוני אוכלוסייה`).

## General rules

- Units: almost all `edu_*` fields are **percentages (0–100)**, rounded to **2 decimals**.
- Missing markers in the source:
  - `..` and `.` → `NaN`
- `-` → `0` **only** for `edu_dropout_pct`; for all other `edu_*` fields → `NaN`
- Rows with `municipal_status == "מועצה אזורית"` (regional councils) are excluded.

## Column set

By default, the script writes a **recommended (compact) set** of columns for analysis.

- Recommended export: `python extract_education_from_p_libud_23.py`
- Full export (includes the full 25–65 attainment ladder): `python extract_education_from_p_libud_23.py --all-columns`

## Columns

### Identifiers

- `settlement_name` — settlement / local authority name (Hebrew), as in the source.
- `settlement_symbol` — numeric settlement / authority code (סמל הרשות); the join key for other datasets.
- `district` — district / מחוז (Hebrew), as in the source.
- `municipal_status` — municipal status / מעמד מוניציפלי (e.g., עירייה / מועצה מקומית).

### Recommended metrics (included by default)

- `edu_dropout_pct` — **student dropout rate (%)** (“נושרים”; source: `אחוז תלמידים נושרים`, total).  
  Note: the source sometimes contains `-`; here it is interpreted as `0`.
- `edu_bagrut_eligibility_pct` — **% eligible for a Bagrut certificate** among 12th‑grade students (source: `אחוז זכאים לתעודת בגרות מבין תלמידי כיתות יב`).
- `edu_bagrut_uni_req_pct` — **% eligible for Bagrut and meeting university threshold requirements** among 12th‑grade students (source: `אחוז זכאים לתעודת בגרות שעמדו בדרישות הסף של האוניברסיטאות מבין תלמידי כיתות יב`).
- `edu_higher_ed_entry_within_8y_pct` — **% entering higher education within 8 years** among 12th‑grade students (source: `אחוז הנכנסים להשכלה גבוהה בתוך 8 שנים בקרב תלמידי יב`).
- `edu_attain_pct_no_info` — % “no education information” among ages 25–65 (source: `אין מידע על השכלה`).

### Derived fields (computed in the script)

- `edu_attain_pct_academic_degree` — **BA + MA + PhD**.  
  If all three values are missing → `NaN`; otherwise the sum over available components.
- `edu_attain_pct_bagrut_or_higher` — **share of ages 25–65 with “Bagrut or higher”**, based on the attainment distribution:  
  `edu_attain_pct_highschool_bagrut + edu_attain_pct_postsecondary_nonacademic + edu_attain_pct_ba + edu_attain_pct_ma + edu_attain_pct_phd`

  Plain‑English interpretation:
  - **Bagrut** (תעודת בגרות) is the Israeli matriculation certificate (high‑school completion with Bagrut).
  - “**Or higher**” includes categories that are above “high school with Bagrut” in the LAMAS classification:  
    post‑secondary **non‑academic** certificate and academic degrees `BA/MA/PhD`.
  - We **do not** include `edu_attain_pct_highschool_unknown_bagrut`, because it explicitly says “unknown if Bagrut”; including it would inflate the metric.

  How to read this metric:
  - It is an approximate **share with confirmed attainment ≥ Bagrut** among ages 25–65 in the settlement.
  - If a settlement has high `edu_attain_pct_no_info` and/or `edu_attain_pct_highschool_unknown_bagrut`, then `edu_attain_pct_bagrut_or_higher` is closer to a **lower bound** (some people may have Bagrut/degree but fall into “no info / unknown”).

  Example: if `highschool_bagrut=20`, `postsecondary_nonacademic=8`, `ba=15`, `ma=5`, `phd=1`, then `edu_attain_pct_bagrut_or_higher = 49` (%).

### Additional columns (only with `--all-columns`)

When running `python extract_education_from_p_libud_23.py --all-columns`, the CSV also includes the detailed attainment ladder (ages 25–65):

- `edu_attain_pct_below_elem` — below completion of primary school (8-year).
- `edu_attain_pct_middle_or_elem_cert` — certificate of middle/primary school completion (8-year).
- `edu_attain_pct_highschool_unknown_bagrut` — completed high school (12 years), unknown whether Bagrut.
- `edu_attain_pct_highschool_no_bagrut` — completed high school without Bagrut.
- `edu_attain_pct_highschool_bagrut` — completed high school with Bagrut.
- `edu_attain_pct_postsecondary_nonacademic` — post‑secondary non‑academic certificate.
- `edu_attain_pct_ba` — Bachelor’s degree.
- `edu_attain_pct_ma` — Master’s degree.
- `edu_attain_pct_phd` — Doctoral degree.
