Education Snapshot

Interactive exploration of education indicators by settlement (CBS/LAMAS). Use the controls to rank settlements, explore relationships, and see distributions.

Dataset

What this is

This presentation is based on p_libud_23.xlsx (sheet: נתונים פיזיים ונתוני אוכלוסייה), exported into education_from_p_libud_23.csv. Values are percentages unless stated otherwise.

Rank settlements (interactive)

Choose a metric, pick “best” or “worst”, and adjust Top N.

Explore relationships (interactive)

Scatter plot by district; marker size can reflect “no info”.

Distributions (interactive)

How do settlements distribute across each metric?

Data quality & missingness (interactive)

Click a bar to pick a metric and instantly see which settlements have missing values. Switch “View” to explore highest/lowest values (e.g., high “No info”).
